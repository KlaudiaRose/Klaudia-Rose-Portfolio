PORTFOLIO - SPACES

My style: Mystical, Creepy, Natural, Warped, Magical, Otherworldly. I'm a composer and multi-instrumentalist interested in collaborating with film makers, animation artists and game designers. Here you will find my updated works, mostly Film soundtrack style. I used the below images to inspire the companion compositions. These compositions correlate to the sound, feeling or story of the image, where the visual and auditory world align. Music is transportational for me, it's exploring new dimensions each time, seeing them for what they are and translating them back to our world, into the language of music.
